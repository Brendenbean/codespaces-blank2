The Komodo Dragon

Komodo Dragons are venomous reptiles that live in southeastern indonesia. The male species can grow up to 10 feet in length and for the women species up to 8 feet. When Komodo dragons hunt they rely on there camouflauge and patience to strike there prey. They jump on there targets dig the claws into there skin and bite them then the posion will kill the target. there are 4 phases to the komodo dragon life cycle
